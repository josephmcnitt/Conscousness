## Immediate Stop — Cease All Imaginal Work

- Disorganization ≥ 4/5 for two consecutive sessions
- Boundary clarity ≤ 2/5 for three consecutive sessions
- Involuntary imagery persisting > 30 min after seal
- Sleep disruption 3+ nights after Veil 5+ work
- Depersonalization or derealization lasting > 1 hour
- Partner reports fear or coercion in dyad

**Action:** Ritual 08; Grade 0 only for 7 days; medical care if needed.

---

## Medical / Psychiatric Stop

- Hallucinations indistinguishable from waking perception without consent
- Suicidal ideation linked to practice content
- Manic episode onset correlated with practice intensification

**Action:** Cease VR entirely; professional care first.

---
