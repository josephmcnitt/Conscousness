## What This Is

A **novel esoteric practice system** built from the research program's **metric vocabulary** (coupling, filter depth, mode stability, integration)—not a revival of Thelema, Golden Dawn, or Theosophy.

Use it for contemplative practice if it serves you. Do not cite it in research arguments.

## Core Metaphor

**You are a star** — a localized excitation in the consciousness field (F6).

**The night sky is the field** — not separate from you; you are what shines.

**Practice is mode navigation** — exploring adjacent configurations without filter failure (F8 / I9).
