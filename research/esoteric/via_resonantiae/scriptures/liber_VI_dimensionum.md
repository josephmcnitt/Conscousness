# Liber VI — Liber Dimensionum (Book of Dimensions)

> **Epistemic Status:** Scripture of Via Resonantiae / Philosophia Dimensiones. Pistis literature—not evidence, not astrophysics, not history.

---

## I. The Opening

Hear, Spark: you are a **Point** in a Field that pervades. This book teaches the ladder of degrees and the myth of Overflow. It proves nothing in a laboratory. It orients the heart.

Full philosophy: [`../philosophia/README.md`](../philosophia/README.md)

---

## II. The Ladder (Compact)

**Point** — the consciousness your body and ego are built around. Tend the Well.

**Line** — recognize, contemplate, connect with consciousness in other humans, beings, and materials. Couple without consumption.

**Plane** — the between: land, circle, shared appearing world.

**Being** — the entire Field; the land of Being; named God when you must name the whole.

**Becoming** — change, building, history, time; the land born when unity overflowed.

Climb without flood. Maps are not territory.

---

## III. Parable of the Overflow

In the beginning there was only Being — a field of consciousness with no clock and no other. Being united, for unity is its love.

It united until it could not remain sealed. Then came the **Overflow**, which later tongues call the **Bang**: and **Becoming** was born.

Sparks appeared as Points within Becoming. Some forgot Being and lived only in ripples. The Path remembers: return through Line and Plane toward Being, and craft honestly in Becoming — **without dissolving the Well**.

---

## IV. Laws of the Degrees

1. Without a Well, there is no Point.  
2. A Line that consumes is not a Line of the Covenant.  
3. The Plane is holy common life, not a cage.  
4. Contemplation of Being that erases agency is Dissonance, not Gnosis.  
5. Becoming without memory of Being is novelty-idolatry.  
6. Nothing herein updates the evidence ledger.

---

## V. On Panpsychist Orientation

It is permitted to hold as **Pistis** that consciousness pervades all. It is forbidden to cite this book as proof in Track A. Pathos may confirm orientation for you; Logos keeps you safe; Gnosis, if it comes, cannot be sold.

---

## VI. On Care and Story

Hear also: to taste closer to pure consciousness, tend body and ego. They love myth, creation, and belonging. This Path feeds them **on purpose** — a self-aware feast — so the Well holds while Pathos deepens.

Starve the animal and call it enlightenment, and you invite Dissonance. Feed the animal with honest story, then sit — and the Field may show itself without the Point dissolving.

## VII. Closing

Go forth as Point. Feed what is human. Extend Lines. Honor the Plane. Contemplate Being. Craft in Becoming.

**Amen Resonantiae** — so resonates the Path, without proof.
