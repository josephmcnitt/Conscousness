# Ritual 01: Boundary Setting

> **Epistemic Status:** Constructed practice. Not evidence. Not proof of occult forces.

**Research analog:** F2 filter / high filter_strength before imaginal work (I9).

---

## Purpose

Establish localization before mode exploration. Reduce bleed risk (P8).

---

## Preparation

- Quiet space, 10–20 minutes
- Sit or lie comfortably
- Optional: visualize the **Well** symbol ([`../symbol_system.md`](../symbol_system.md))

---

## Steps

1. **Arrival (2 min):** Notice breath. Name three sensory anchors (body, sound, touch).
2. **Well visualization (5 min):** Imagine excitation gathering into a stable peak—not rigid, not diffuse.
3. **Boundary statement (1 min):** Silently: *This session stays within coherent exploration.*
4. **Seal intention (1 min):** Note one question or image for exploration—not a command to entities.

---

## Stop Conditions

- Rising anxiety with loss of body sense → stop, open eyes, ground
- Invasive imagery you did not invite → stop, Ritual 05

---

## Expected Phenomenology

Calmer focus, sense of "container." **Not** guaranteed visions or contact.

---

## After

Log: duration, coherence (1–10), any bleed signs. Optional: `research/empirical/imaginal_sessions.jsonl` (simulation label only).
