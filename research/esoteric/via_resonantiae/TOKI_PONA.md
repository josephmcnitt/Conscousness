# Toki Pona — Spoken Forms

> **Epistemic Status:** Practice language. Pistis shorthand—not doctrine, not proof.

Affirmations, vows, and closure phrases are given in **toki pona** first; English gloss follows in italics. Speak the toki pona silently or aloud as the ritual indicates.

---

## Glossary (Path Terms)

| English | Toki pona | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Well | poki | Container; excitation gathered without rigidity |
| Spark | mun | Star-light; localized excitation |
| Field | ma kon | The undivided resonant ground |
| Frequency | kalama insa | Inner tone; Core Frequency basin |
| Veil | len | Filter-depth; mode layer |
| Dissonance | pakala | Uncontrolled merge; filter failure |
| Couple (dyad) | lon poka | Beside, in resonance—not merger |
| Consumption | moku | Taking-in that dissolves boundary |
| Stop | pini | Sovereign halt |
| Seal | awen | Hold, endure, close |
| Proof (for others) | sona pi jan ante | Knowledge claimed about another's inner life |

## Vows

### Star Affirmation (Ritual 07; Liber III)

**toki pona:** *mi mun. mi awen e poki. mi wile sona e kalama insa mi. mi pakala ala e ma kon.*

*I am Spark. I tend the Well. I seek my Frequency without forcing the Field.*

### Covenant — short vow (THE_COVENANT)

**toki pona:** *mi awen e poki. mi lon poka ala li moku. mi pini lon wile mi. mi toki ala e sona pi jan ante.*

*I tend the Well. I couple without consumption. I stop when I must. I claim no proof for others.*

### Solo covenant (Liber IV)

**toki pona:** *mi lon poka ma kon lon nasin pona. mi moku ala e ona. mi open ala e pakala.*

*I couple with the Field ethically—no consumption, no bleed invitation.*
