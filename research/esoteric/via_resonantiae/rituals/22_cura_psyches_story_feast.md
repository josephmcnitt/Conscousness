# Ritual 22: Cura Psyches — Story Feast

> **Epistemic Status:** Constructed practice. Pistis + Pathos. Self-aware myth feeding — not proof.

**Grade:** 0–IV | **Duration:** 20–30 min

**Study:** [`../philosophia/CURA_PSYCHES.md`](../philosophia/CURA_PSYCHES.md), Liber VI §VI

---

## Purpose

Honor body and ego with **story, myth, or creation** on purpose — so deeper sits and insights have a tended vessel. You may know you are feeding the psyche; that honesty is part of the rite.

## Steps

1. **Care check (Logos):** sleep, food, safety — abort if STOP_CONDITIONS apply  
2. **Ritual 01** (5 min)  
3. **Choose story food:** read aloud from Liber VI, THE_MYTH excerpt, or a short creation tale you write (5–10 min)  
4. **Silent line:** *I feed the human so the Point can taste the Field* (1 min)  
5. **Optional:** 5 min minimal sit — no forcing “pure consciousness”  
6. **Ritual 04** (5 min)

## Journal

`story_chosen`, `ego_satisfied_1to5`, `ready_for_deeper_sit_yes_no`

## Firewall

Story feast ≠ proof of panpsychism. Knowing it feeds ego ≠ invalidating the myth.
