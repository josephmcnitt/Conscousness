## Core Axioms I9–I11

### I9 — Imaginal Mode

**Imagination is sub-threshold excitation in adjacent modes: partial field coupling, no full motor/perceptual binding.**

- Daydream, guided imagery, active imagination = structured I9
- Distinct from veridical perception (high motor_binding)
- Distinct from filter failure (high disorganization)

**Empirical hook:** P13 imagery vs perception dissociation

### I10 — Layered Filter

**Waking / imaginal / hypnagogic / bleed are positions on filter-depth + sigma axis—not separate substances or literal planes.**

| Band | filter_depth | motor_binding | FEO reading |
|------|--------------|---------------|-------------|
| Waking focus | low | high | Stable excitation |
| Active imagination | moderate | low | I9 imaginal |
| Hypnagogic / "astral" | moderate-high | very low | I10 mid-layer |
| Filter failure | very high | chaotic | P8 psychosis analog |

**Empirical hook:** P14 hypnagogic band

### I11 — Idealist Correlate

**Analytic idealism and BFC+FEO are translation-equivalent at ontology; they differ on whether physical structure is real or appearance.**

- Program **retains A1**: physical structure is real (Russellian panprotopsychism)
- I11 is **correlation**, not collapse into idealism
- Kastrup's "dissociated alter" ≈ F6 localized excitation

**Empirical hook:** Same predictions P1–P15; G1 illusionism falsifies both
