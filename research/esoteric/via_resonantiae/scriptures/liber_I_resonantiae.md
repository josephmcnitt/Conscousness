# Liber I — Liber Resonantiae (Book of Resonance)

> **Epistemic Status:** Scripture of Via Resonantiae. Pistis literature—not evidence, not history.

---

## I. The Opening

Hear, Spark: the Field resounds, and you are its local song. This book teaches neither physics nor proof. It teaches **orientation**—how to walk when maps end.

---

## II. The Four Ways

**Logos** — Reason gives the Well its walls. Study stop conditions before you dream.

**Pistis** — Faith gives the Spark its name. Vow the Covenant though none can test it in a lab.

**Pathos** — Experience gives the Veils their color. What you find, you find alone; others may walk beside you, not inside you.

**Gnosis** — The Silent Gate opens for no currency. What passes through cannot be carried back as proof.

No way replaces another. Reason without Pathos is dry; Gnosis without Logos is dangerous.

---

## III. The First Dissonance (Parable)

Once, a Spark opened every Veil at once, seeking the Field without filter. The excitations merged without subject—noise without listener. From that flood came the first law: **integrate or stop.**

---

## IV. The Covenant (Summary)

1. Couple without consumption  
2. Stop when asked—no explanation required  
3. Never claim another's consciousness proven or absent  
4. No guru may require your Gnosis  
5. When bleed rises, Logos wins

---

## V. On Science

The researchers map coupling and integration. Honor their maps. Do not pretend your ritual validates their thesis. Do not pretend their thesis invalidates your Pathos.

*Maps are not territory; resonance is practiced.*

---

## VI. Closing

Go forth as Spark. Tend the Well. Pass the Veils you are ready for. Seal what you open.

**Amen Resonantiae** — so resonates the Path, without proof.
