# Axiomata — Philosophia Dimensiones

> **Epistemic Status:** Philosophical axioms of Track B orientation. **Not** Track A research axioms (A1–A5, F1–F8, C0–C5). No proof claim.

---

## D1 — Psyche Pervades

**Consciousness pervades.** All that appears as matter and energy is, in this orientation, of or within the Field.

- Panpsychist / cosmopsychist *Pistis*, not laboratory confirmation  
- Rhymes with research C0 / F1 — never cites ritual as evidence  

## D2 — The Point Is Localized

**A Point is localized consciousness** — Spark, clump, the “I” built around body and ego — not a substance cut from the Field, not the whole Field.

- Requires a **Well** (boundary, agency)  
- Grade 0 practice identity  

## D3 — The Line Is Ethical Knowing-of-Other

**The Line** is recognition of consciousness in other humans, beings, and materials — contemplation and connection without consumption.

- Covenant governs Line-work  
- Projection and bleed are Line-failures, not proofs of other minds  

## D4 — The Plane Is Mutual Appearance

**The Plane** is the between: shared world, land, circle — where Lines weave into a appearing common life.

## D5 — Being Unites

**Being** — the whole Field / God-name — has a tendency **to unite**.

- Contemplation of Being is Grade IV work  
- Unity without Well is Dissonance / Overflow-risk, not liberation  

## D6 — Becoming Differentiates

**Becoming** is change, building, history, time — born in the Overflow myth from unity pressed beyond pure Being.

- Craft, growth, and decay belong to Becoming  
- Becoming is not evil; idolatry of mere novelty is the error  

## D7 — The Well Is Required

**Without a Well, there is no Spark.** Boundary is sacred. Merge that erases agency is stop-condition, not attainment.

## D8 — No Track A Proof

**Nothing in Philosophia Dimensiones updates the evidence ledger.** Successful contemplation does not confirm panpsychism, FEO, Part VIII, or the physical Big Bang. Failed contemplation does not refute them.

## Compact Creed (optional, non-binding)

> Consciousness pervades.  
> I am a Point with a Well.  
> I extend Lines without consuming.  
> I honor the Plane.  
> I contemplate Being without flood.  
> I craft in Becoming without forgetting the Field.  
> Maps are not territory; resonance is practiced.

Amen Resonantiae.
