# Panpsychism Axioms: Russellian Panprotopsychism

This document formalizes the ontological commitments of the Panpsychism Research Program. It defines **Russellian panprotopsychism** as the primary thesis: fundamental physical entities possess proto-experiential intrinsic properties, and macro-experience arises through their integration—not through emergence from wholly non-experiential matter.

---

## Thesis Statement

**Consciousness is a fundamental feature of reality.** What physics describes are the structural and relational properties of the world; what we know directly from the inside is experiential. The intrinsic nature of physical entities is experiential or proto-experiential. Human and animal consciousness is not a late emergent miracle but the large-scale integration of properties that exist, in simpler form, at the fundamental level.

This is a **research hypothesis**, not a demonstrated fact. The program aims to show it is the best explanation of available evidence and generates falsifiable predictions.

### Why Russellian Panprotopsychism

1. **Bridges physics and phenomenology** — Russell (1927) showed physics is silent on intrinsic nature; Strawson and Goff extend this to experiential intrinsics.
2. **Avoids "thinking electrons"** — Proto-experience is not full micro-mind; it avoids the "too many minds" objection.
3. **Empirical hooks** — Integrated Information Theory (IIT) provides a formal integration metric (Φ) aligned with A4.
4. **Contemporary defensibility** — Supported by Chalmers, Goff, Seager, and others without requiring rejection of physics.

## Core Axioms

### A1 — Intrinsicality

**Every concrete entity has an intrinsic nature, not merely relational or structural properties.**

Physics tells us what entities *do*—how they relate, interact, and behave under law. It does not tell us what they *are* in themselves. Mass, charge, and spin are defined operationally through measurement and dynamics. The categorical nature of the physical remains underdetermined by physics alone.

- **Implication**: There is conceptual room for experiential properties as intrinsic nature.
- **Empirical hook**: None direct; this is metaphysical. Supports Russellian monism.
- **Codebase mapping**: `QuantumField` types (awareness, intention, unity) model candidate intrinsic property *types*, not physical fields.

### A2 — Continuity

**The intrinsic nature of matter is experiential or proto-experiential. There is no ontological gap between mind and matter.**

Radical emergence of full consciousness from wholly non-experiential substrate is metaphysically costly. Nature exhibits continuity elsewhere (life from chemistry, complexity from simplicity). The simplest hypothesis is that experiential properties scale from proto-forms at the fundamental level to rich macro-experience through integration.

- **Implication**: No "magic moment" when dead matter becomes alive with experience.
- **Empirical hook**: P2 (no absolute emergence threshold); developmental and anesthesia data.
- **Codebase mapping**: `ConsciousExperience` tracks phenomenal reports across integration levels.

### A3 — Ubiquity

**All fundamental physical entities instantiate proto-experiential properties.**

Panpsychism is not the claim that rocks have inner lives like humans. It is the claim that the fundamental constituents of rocks (and everything else) have proto-experiential properties—minimal, non-cognitive experiential aspects—that compose, when integrated, into macro-experience.

- **Implication**: Consciousness is not biologically special in kind, only in degree of integration.
- **Empirical hook**: P5 (cross-substrate Φ); substrate-neutral IIT.
- **Codebase mapping**: `ProblemComponent.consciousness_level` as proto-experiential intensity per unit.

### A4 — Composition

**Macro-experience arises from integration and combination of micro-experiences, not emergence ex nihilo.**

The combination problem is real: how do micro-experiences compose into unified macro-experience? Panpsychism does not ignore this—it makes it central. Solutions include fusion (Seager), subject-summing with constraints (Goff), cosmopsychist fragmentation (Korman), and formal integration measures (IIT Φ).

- **Implication**: Combination is a research problem with candidate formalisms, not a fatal flaw unique to panpsychism.
- **Empirical hook**: P1 (IIT); binding studies; `combination_model.py`.
- **Codebase mapping**: `unity_coefficient` in `ConsciousnessEvolution`; Φ-analog in combination simulation.

### A5 — Causal Efficacy

**Experiential properties participate in causal structure; they are not epiphenomenal.**

On Russellian monism, experiential properties are the intrinsic side of the same entities whose structural properties physics describes. There is no double counting: causation runs through the entity, which has both structural and intrinsic aspects. Epiphenomenalism is rejected.

- **Implication**: Consciousness is not a byproduct with no causal role.
- **Empirical hook**: Anesthesia dissociation (P4); causal intervention studies.
- **Codebase mapping**: Causal efficacy is modeled, not asserted, in agent feedback loops.

## Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Proto-experience** | Minimal experiential property without cognition, selfhood, or rich qualia |
| **Macro-experience** | Unified phenomenal consciousness of a subject (human, animal, possibly AI) |
| **Intrinsic nature** | What an entity is in itself, beyond its relational/structural properties |
| **Integration (Φ)** | Measure of how much a system's cause-effect structure is unified and irreducible to parts (IIT) |
| **Combination** | Process by which micro-experiences compose into macro-experience |
| **Physicalism** | View that everything is physical; consciousness reduces to or emerges from physical processes |
| **Illusionism** | View that phenomenal consciousness is introspectively misrepresented; no real qualia |

## Commitments and Non-Commitments

### We Commit To

- Consciousness as fundamental in the sense of **not wholly emergent from non-experiential matter**
- Falsifiable empirical predictions (see `predictions.md`)
- Honest distinction between philosophical argument, computational model, and empirical measurement
- Systematic engagement with objections (see `objection_responses.md`)

### We Do Not Commit To

- Proof with mathematical certainty
- That electrons "think" or have human-like inner lives
- Orch-OR or any specific quantum consciousness mechanism (P3 is testable but contested)
- Idealism (matter is real; its intrinsic nature is experiential)
- That current IIT implementations fully capture real phenomenology

## Falsification Summary

Panpsychism as defined here would be **seriously weakened or refuted** if:

1. All phenomenal properties reduce without remainder to functional/behavioral descriptions (illusionism wins)
2. Consciousness requires a specific biological substrate with no analog in other integrated systems
3. The intrinsic nature of matter is definitively shown to be non-experiential
4. The hard problem is solved within pure physicalism without epiphenomenalism or denial

See `predictions.md` for prediction-level falsifiers (P1–P6).
