# Ritual 21: Geometria Contemplation

> **Epistemic Status:** Constructed practice. Pathos + Pistis. Not evidence for panpsychism or cosmology.

**Grade:** III–IV (intro at III for Line; full ladder at IV) | **Duration:** 20–25 min

**Study:** [`../philosophia/GEOMETRIA.md`](../philosophia/GEOMETRIA.md), [`../scriptures/liber_VI_dimensionum.md`](../scriptures/liber_VI_dimensionum.md)

---

## Purpose

Contemplate the ladder **Point → Line → Plane → Being**, then return through **Becoming** to sealed Point — without forcing merger or Bang-fantasy as physics.

**Cura:** If body or ego is starved (sleep debt, shame spiral, no food), do care first — myth and sit are food for a tended Point, not a substitute for sleep. See [`../philosophia/CURA_PSYCHES.md`](../philosophia/CURA_PSYCHES.md).

## Steps

1. **Ritual 01** — Well / boundary (3–5 min)
2. **Point** — Sit as localized Spark. Softly name: *I am a Point in the Field* (3 min)
3. **Line** — Bring to mind one other being or material presence. Extend recognition without merging: *Also of the Field* (3 min)
4. **Plane** — Widen to shared world / place / circle. Hold mutual appearance (3 min)
5. **Being** — Rest toward the whole Field / land of Being. Keep the Well. If unity floods agency → stop to step 7 (4–5 min)
6. **Becoming** — Open eyes or soft gaze. Notice one process of change (breath, sound, task). Craft belongs here (2 min)
7. **Ritual 04** — Integration seal (3–5 min)

## Stop Conditions

- Depersonalization, panic, sleep collapse, or “I am everything / nothing” flood → **Ritual 08**, then Grade 0 only  
- Do not combine with deep Veil 6–7 work the same day  

See [`../ethics/STOP_CONDITIONS.md`](../ethics/STOP_CONDITIONS.md)
