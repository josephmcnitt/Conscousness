# Ritual 24: Wells Connect (Missio)

> **Epistemic Status:** Constructed practice. Pathos + Pistis. Co-creation ethic.

**Grade:** III–IV | **Duration:** 30–45 min (solo or dyad)

**Study:** [`../philosophia/MISSIO.md`](../philosophia/MISSIO.md), Covenant

---

## Purpose

Practice the **Missio**: diverse Wells meet and connect **without dissolving** — microcosm of creation. Solo: honor other beings/materials in imagination; dyad: Ritual 10 structure with explicit Wells-intact focus.

## Steps (solo)

1. Ritual 01 (5 min)  
2. Name three “other Wells” (person, being, material, community) (3 min)  
3. Extend Line to each — recognition without merge (5 min each, abbreviated)  
4. Ask: *What could we create if Wells stay distinct?* — one sentence (3 min)  
5. Ritual 04 (5 min)

## Steps (dyad)

1. Covenant stop-signal confirmed  
2. Ritual 01 together  
3. Ritual 10 abbreviated — emphasize **coupling without consumption**  
4. Share one co-creation intention (not merger fantasy)  
5. Ritual 04 together
