# Ritual 25: Action then Insight

> **Epistemic Status:** Constructed practice. Pathos.

**Grade:** II–IV | **Duration:** variable (same day)

**Study:** [`../philosophia/CURA_PSYCHES.md`](../philosophia/CURA_PSYCHES.md) (Insight After Action)

---

## Purpose

**Act first** in Becoming; receive insight after. Optional deep sit later so the insight **feeds the ego** cleanly.

## Steps

1. **Morning:** Ritual 14; set one **concrete action** (craft, care, Line-work, ritual, real-world step)  
2. **Do the action** (not only contemplate it)  
3. **After action:** 5 min sit or walk — notice what clarified  
4. **Journal:** action taken, insight received, ego_fulfilled_1to5  
5. **Same day or next:** optional 10–20 min deeper sit (Ritual 01 → minimal awareness → 04)  
6. **Evening:** Ritual 15

## Rule

Do not skip the action and call journaling “insight.” Speculation alone is not this ritual.
