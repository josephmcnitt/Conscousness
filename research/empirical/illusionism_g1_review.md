# Illusionism and G1: Desk Review

Part V synthesis for **global falsifier G1** — the strongest threat to the entire program.

---

## G1 Statement

> All phenomenal properties reduce without remainder to functional/behavioral descriptions.

If true, panpsychism, FEO, IEO, and FEP practice (which assumes experiential realism) are undermined.

---

## Key Positions

### Frankish — Illusionism

- Qualia as introspective illusion; "seeming" is real, phenomenal properties are not
- Must explain **persistent structured seeming** (flow, pain, insight)

### Dennett — Quining Qualia

- Qualia replaced by multiple drafts / access consciousness
- Phenomenology is heterophenomenology — reports only

### Humphrey — Sentition

- Sensory affect as action-oriented signal; richness without private qualia

---

## What Illusionism Must Explain

| Phenomenon | Challenge for G1 |
|------------|------------------|
| Suffering | Why care if nothing real hurts? |
| Flow | Structured seeming with neural correlates (P10) |
| Insight | Pre-report reorganization (P11) |

If access-only models explain all without loss, G1 gains support.
