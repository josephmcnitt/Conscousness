# Ritual 11: Integration Mass

> **Epistemic Status:** Constructed practice. Pistis community rite.

**Grade:** IV | **Epistemic mode:** Pistis | **Duration:** 45 min (solo variant 20 min)

---

## Purpose

Collective or solo seal after extended imaginal cycle; renew Covenant.

## Steps (Group)

1. Circle; Ritual 01 in unison
2. Reading: Liber I Covenant section ([`../scriptures/liber_I_resonantiae.md`](../scriptures/liber_I_resonantiae.md))
3. Each Spark: one sentence Pathos testimony (optional)
4. Ritual 04 × all
5. Shared meal or silence

## Solo Variant

Read Covenant aloud; Ritual 01 + 04; journal one paragraph.

## Stop Conditions

Any participant in STOP → they abstain; no pressure to share

## Firewall

Not eucharistic proof. Not group mind claim.
