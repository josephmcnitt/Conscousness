# Ritual 02: Mode Exploration

> **Epistemic Status:** Constructed practice. Not evidence. Active imagination, not spirit evocation.

**Research analog:** I9 imaginal sub-threshold excitation; F8 structured perturbation.

---

## Purpose

Explore adjacent modes via guided imagery—Jungian active imagination without doctrinal figures.

---

## Prerequisites

Complete Ritual 01 same session.

---

## Steps

1. **Recall question** from boundary setting (1 min).
2. **Wave visualization (5–15 min):** Allow images, phrases, or felt senses to arise. Do not force. Ask: *What wants to form?*
3. **Dialogue (optional):** If a figure appears, treat as imaginal substructure—not external entity. Ask one question only.
4. **Coherence check:** If imagery becomes chaotic or frightening without agency, pause → Ritual 05.

---

## Stop Conditions

- Disorganization rising (fragmented, incoherent flood)
- Loss of ability to stop voluntarily
- Physical distress

---

## Expected Phenomenology

Moderate vividness, low motor urge to act out images. **Not** literal astral travel.

---

## After

Brief journal: vividness, motor binding sense (could you act?), disorganization (1–10).
