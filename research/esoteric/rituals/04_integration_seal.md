# Ritual 04: Integration Seal

> **Epistemic Status:** Constructed practice. Not evidence. Grounding after imaginal work.

**Research analog:** P11 mode transition seal; post-insight integration.

---

## Purpose

Consolidate session content without re-opening bleed. Return excitation to stable basin.

---

## When

After Ritual 02 or any imaginal session.

---

## Steps

1. **Summary (2 min):** One sentence of what arose—no elaborate mythmaking.
2. **Body anchor (3 min):** Feet, hands, breath; feel weight.
3. **Well re-seal (2 min):** Visualize excitation settling into stable peak.
4. **Closure phrase (optional):** *The wave returns to the well.*

---

## Stop Conditions

N/A — this ritual is always safe to perform.

---

## Expected Phenomenology

Reduced vividness of session imagery, restored ordinary agency.

---

## After

Note whether insight feels integrated (1–10) vs floating/ungrounded.
