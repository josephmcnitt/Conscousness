# Epistemic Charter — Via Resonantiae

> **This charter is binding on all Path material.** Track B never updates research evidence.

**Motto:** *Maps are not territory; resonance is practiced.*

---

## Why This Exists

Parts I–VIII of the Consciousness Research Program pursue **Logos** (reason): communicable, publicly testable maps. The hard problem shows reason alone may not close the gap between structure and what-it-is-like.

**Via Resonantiae** (The Resonant Path) addresses what remains through complementary modes—without pretending any mode proves what another cannot.

**Also:** deeper minimal phenomenology often needs a tended body and ego; body and ego hunger for story. Esotericism here is a **self-aware** attempt to fulfill that hunger so panpsychist practice can be inhabited by a human psyche — see [`philosophia/CURA_PSYCHES.md`](philosophia/CURA_PSYCHES.md). Myths feed the Well; they do not update the evidence ledger.

## The Four Ways

| Way | Greek | Communicable | Verifiable | Role on the Path |
|-----|-------|--------------|------------|------------------|
| **Logos** | Reason | Yes | Publicly | Safety, ethics, maps, coordination with science |
| **Pistis** | Faith | Yes | No | Community vows, myth as orienting story, ritual continuity |
| **Pathos** | Experience | No | Personally | Practice confirms or falsifies *for you* |
| **Gnosis** | Gnosis | No | No | Non-transferable opening; never proof for others |

### Logos

Use research vocabulary as **maps**: veils, resonance, integration, bleed. Logos sets stop conditions, reads [`STOP_CONDITIONS.md`](ethics/STOP_CONDITIONS.md), and never claims ritual metrics detect consciousness.

### Pistis

Myth, scripture, vows, and initiation rites orient the practitioner. Pistis is **communicable but not verifiable**—you may trust the Covenant without requiring others to share your faith.

### Pathos

What you discover in practice is **yours**. Others may relate; they cannot verify your interior. Journal in Pathos mode; do not demand agreement.

### Gnosis

Rare, non-transferable openings may occur at Grade V. Gnosis **cannot be taught, sold, or used as authority**. Anti-guru clause: no one may require your gnosis or deny your lack of it.

## Hard Rules

1. VR **never** updates [`../../evidence_ledger.json`](../../evidence_ledger.json)
2. VR **never** cites ritual success as support for panpsychism, FEO, IEO, or HPP tracks
3. If practice contradicts safety (bleed, psychosis risk, sleep collapse), **Logos wins**
4. Gnosis claims cannot be demanded of others
5. FEP / VR practice does not prove spirits, planes, or entities
6. Research wins when practice and falsifiers conflict
