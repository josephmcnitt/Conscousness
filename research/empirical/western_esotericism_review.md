## Purpose

## Theosophy (Blavatsky)

**Key claims:** Planes (physical, astral, mental, buddhic); subtle bodies; thought-forms.

**IEO mapping:**

| Theosophical | IEO band |
|--------------|----------|
| Physical plane | High motor_binding, deep filter |
| Astral plane | I10 mid-layer: moderate filter_depth, high vividness |
| Mental plane | Narrow sigma, high stability |
| Thought-forms | Structured I9 excitations (not independent substances) |

**Use:** Historical vocabulary for filter-depth table in [`IMAGINATION_AND_THE_ASTRAL.md`](../IMAGINATION_AND_THE_ASTRAL.md).

## Golden Dawn

**Key claims:** Pathworking, visualization, grade initiations, Middle Pillar.

**IEO mapping:**

- Pathworking = **guided I9** with narrative scaffold (structured F8)
- Middle Pillar = sequential filter calibration (boundary discipline)
- **P15 hook:** Structured GD-style work vs unstructured trance

**Use:** Inspiration for FEP ritual structure—not doctrinal authority.

## Jung: Active Imagination

**Key claims:** Dialogue with imaginal figures; collective unconscious as shared field-analog.

**IEO mapping:**

- Active imagination = structured I9
- Over-identification with figures without integration = P8 risk
- **P13/P14:** Imagery with partial perceptual overlap, low motor binding

**Use:** Strongest empirical-adjacent bridge in esoteric literature.

4. Collapsing Track A (research) into Track B (FEP practice)
