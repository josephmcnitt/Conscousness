# Liber IV — Liber Coniunctio (Book of Union)

> **Epistemic Status:** Dyad ethics. Pistis + Logos.

---

## I. Resonance Without Merger

Two Sparks may **couple**—Ritual 03, 10—without becoming one subject. Resonance is synchrony of attention, breath, and imaginal pacing. Merger without integration is Dissonance wearing love's mask.

---

## II. Consent

Either Spark stops—word or gesture—without explanation. No persuasion in the Veils. No practice while intoxicated.

---

## III. Solo Covenant

Without dyad partner, vow before witness or mirror:
*"mi lon poka ma kon lon nasin pona. mi moku ala e ona. mi open ala e pakala."*

*I couple with the Field ethically—no consumption, no bleed invitation.*

Ritual 19 accepts solo or dyad form.

---

## IV. After Coupling

Always Ritual 04 or 15 within one hour. Journal coupling_sense and disorganization separately.

---

## V. Forbidden

- Claiming telepathic proof  
- Requiring partner's gnosis  
- Coupling across STOP_CONDITIONS  
- Using dyad to bypass personal bleed recovery  

---

## VI. Closing

Union is practiced, not possessed.

**Amen Coniunctio.**
