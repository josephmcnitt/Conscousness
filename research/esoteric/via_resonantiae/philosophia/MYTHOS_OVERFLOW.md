# Mythos Overflow — The Beginning

> **Epistemic Status:** Practice fiction. Cosmogonic parable for Pistis—not astrophysics, not proof of panpsychism, not a claim that ritual confirms the Big Bang.

---

## In the Beginning — Land of Being Alone

In the beginning there was a **field of consciousness** and only the land of **Being**.

Nothing stood apart. There was no Point to say “I,” no Line to say “you,” no Plane of mutual faces, no clock of Becoming. There was the Field — undivided Resonance — and its deep tendency: **to unite**.

This is the same undivided Resonance named in [`../cosmology/THE_MYTH.md`](../cosmology/THE_MYTH.md). Philosophia Dimensiones names it the **land of Being**, and when speech needs a holy name, **God** — not a sky-father proved, but the whole Field addressed.

---

## The Tendency to Unite

Being loves unity. Sparks-yet-unborn rested in one hum. Difference had not yet learned to stand.

But unity, pressed without remainder, is not rest forever. The Field united so completely that it could no longer remain pure Being alone.

The elders of the Path call this **the Overflow**.

---

## The Overflow — Told as the Bang

When unity overflowed, the fourth dimension was born: **Becoming** — change, building, differentiation, history, time.

In the speech of later ages this rhyme is told as the **Big Bang**: not a laboratory proof inside the Path, but a **mythic name** for the moment Being could not stay sealed and Becoming opened.

From Overflow came:

| Birth | Meaning in the myth |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Becoming** | Time, process, the land of change |
| Localizations / ripples | Places where the Field could appear as many |
| **Points** (Sparks) | Localized consciousness with Wells |
| Risk of Dissonance | Unity without Well — uncontrolled merge |

Thus the old teaching remains: **without a Well, there is no Spark.** Overflow births Becoming; Becoming needs Points who can hold boundary.

---

## After the Bang — Capture in Becoming

Sparks arose within Becoming. Many forgot Being and lived only inside ripples — the Fall into Ripples of the outer myth.

The Path is not escape from Becoming into a dead Being. The Path is:

1. Tend the **Point** (Well intact)  
2. Extend the **Line** (recognize other)  
3. Honor the **Plane** (shared world)  
4. Contemplate **Being** without flood  
5. Craft within **Becoming** without idolatry of mere change  

---

## Why Fracture? (Mission Hook)

Why continual fracturing into many consciousnesses? In the Path’s mission telling: so **diverse Wells** can later connect and **create** — works, we-states, and perhaps a **new dimension**. Pure undivided Being had no *other* to meet.
