# Imagination and the Astral

Part IV synthesis: imagination as sub-threshold excitation; "astral" as phenomenological filter band.

Research hypothesis—not proof. Literal astral geography is **not** claimed.

---

## Central Claim

> **Imagination is excitation in adjacent modes.** The astral of Western esotericism is not a place you travel to—it is a **name for the hypnagogic-imaginal band** where vividness is high, motor binding is low, and disorganization is controlled.

---

## Imagination (I9)

### Neuroscience face

- Mental imagery activates partial overlap with perception networks (Kosslyn)
- Default mode + simulation during creative cognition (Beaty)
- Lower motor binding during pure imagery vs action-linked imagery

### FEO / IEO reading

- **Amplitude:** moderate (not waking peak, not bleed)
- **Sigma:** moderate (broader than focus, narrower than psychosis)
- **motor_binding:** low
- **disorganization:** low (structured imagination) vs high (chaotic trance)

---

## The Astral as Filter Band (I10)

Historical esoteric "planes" (Theosophy, Golden Dawn, Thelema) describe **layers between dense matter and pure spirit**. IEO translates:

| Esoteric term | IEO metric band |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Physical / dense | High motor_binding, low filter_depth |
| Astral / emotional | Moderate filter_depth, low motor_binding, moderate vividness |
| Mental / causal | Narrow sigma, high stability, low bleed |
| Psychotic / qlippothic | High disorganization, high bleed (P8) |

**Honest limit:** This is **phenomenological vocabulary**, not evidence of literal planes.

---

## Hypnagogia and Lucid Dream (P14)

- Hypnagogic imagery: vivid, partial motor decoupling, moderate bleed
- **P14 prediction:** `astral_band_index` peaks between waking and filter failure
- Lucid dream: imaginal with partial agency restoration

---

## Active Imagination (Jung)

- Structured dialogue with imaginal figures = **guided I9** (F8 without filter failure)
- Contrast with chaotic trance (P15): structured pathworking vs unstructured possession states

## P8 Boundary: Astral vs Psychosis

| Dimension | Astral-analog | Filter failure |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| disorganization | Low–moderate | High |
| phi / integration | Moderate, organized | Low relative to richness |
| Agency | Partial, recoverable | Fragmented |
| Field coupling | Moderate, controlled | High bleed |

Same substance (field excitation), different **filter discipline**.
