## Summary Creed of Dialogue

We **borrow images** from Plato, Valentinian forgetfulness, process thought, and physics vocabulary.  
We **reject** false lineage, hatred of matter, entity ontology, and proof-from-ritual.  
We **orient** toward a panpsychist Field in which Points climb Lines through Plane toward Being while crafting in Becoming.
