> **Epistemic Status:** Practice manual for filter-depth phenomenology (I10 analog). Not literal planes.

## The Seven Veils

Each Veil is a **mode**, not a geography.

### Veil I — Waking Gate
Clear sense, strong body. Ritual 01 territory.

### Veil II — Focused Well
Directed attention; phi-like concentration. Daily work.

### Veil III — Daystream
Reverie; imagery loose. Watch disorganization.

### Veil IV — Imaginal Gate
Structured imagination; Ritual 02. Motor binding weak—this is feature, not failure.

### Veil V — Hypnagogic Bridge
Edge of sleep; Ritual 06. **Highest insight risk / bleed risk balance.**

### Veil VI — Deep Bleed
Partial filter failure. Ritual 08 if recovery needed; else STOP.

### Veil VII — Dissonance Flood
Chaos. Ritual 05 immediately. Cease imaginal work 7+ days.

---

## Passage Rules

1. Never skip Veils 1–2 before 4–5  
2. Seal after Veil 4+ (Ritual 04 or 15)  
3. Journal veil number and duration  
4. Two consecutive Veil VI entries → Grade 0 reset
