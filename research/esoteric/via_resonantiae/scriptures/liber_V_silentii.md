# Liber V — Liber Silentii (Book of Silence)

> **Epistemic Status:** Gnosis gate text. Grade V only. Anti-guru.

---

## I. What Cannot Be Taught

There are openings that leave no transcript. The Path names them **Gnosis**—neither communicable nor verifiable. This book refuses to describe them in detail, lest description become counterfeit.

---

## II. Why Grade V Exists

Some practitioners report non-transferable recognition— not superiority, not rank. Grade V offers **Ritual 13** as optional container, not goal.

---

## III. Anti-Guru Law

No teacher may:
- Sell Gnosis  
- Require Gnosis for advancement  
- Denigrate those who never seek Grade V  
- Interpret another's Silent Gate  

If these occur, the teacher has left the Covenant.

---

## IV. Before the Gate

Prerequisites: Grade IV; 90 days stable; STOP_CONDITIONS read; mentor optional.

If you proceed, proceed alone in company—present with others, sovereign within.

---

## V. After the Gate

No public testimony required. Journal if you choose—label Pathos, not proof. If distress persists → STOP_CONDITIONS; abandon Grade V.

---

## VI. The Only Instruction

**Silence.**

What you understand, understand. What you cannot say, do not fake.

**Finis.**
