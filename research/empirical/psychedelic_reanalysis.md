# Psychedelic States: Entropy vs. Integration Reanalysis

Desk synthesis for **Prediction P6** (psychedelic expansion via integration bandwidth). Compares entropic brain theory with integration/connectivity findings.

**Program relevance**: Panpsychism predicts psychedelic richness may reflect expanded integration of proto-experiential access, not mere noise.

---

## Executive Summary

| Framework | Claim | P6 Implication |
|-----------|-------|----------------|
| Entropic brain (Carhart-Harris) | Psychedelics increase neural entropy/disorder | Challenges P6 if entropy alone |
| Functional connectivity (Petri, Barrett) | Psychedelics increase global connectivity | Supports P6 integration expansion |
| Reconciliation | Increased entropy *with* reorganized connectivity | P6 *Mixed* — both structure and disorder |

**Overall P6 status**: *Mixed* — connectivity increases support integration expansion; entropic brain complicates simple P6 story.

## 1. Entropic Brain Theory

### Carhart-Harris et al. (2014). *Frontiers in Human Neuroscience*, 8, 20.

**Claims**:
- Primary consciousness (primitive, emotional) vs. secondary (ego-bound, high-level).
- Psychedelics increase entropy, relaxing constraints of secondary consciousness.
- "Disorder" increases phenomenal richness.

**Panpsychist reading**:
- If richness = noise only, P6 falsifier P6-F1 gains support.
- If entropy reflects **release of integration constraints** allowing broader micro-experiential access, compatible with panpsychism.

**Critical issue**: Entropy metrics (e.g., LZc) may increase while **specific integration structures** also increase— not mutually exclusive.

---

## 2. Integration and Connectivity Evidence

### Petri et al. (2014). *Journal of the Royal Society Interface*, 11(101), 20140873.

**Design**: fMRI functional networks under psilocybin.

**Findings**:
- Increased global functional connectivity.
- Persistent homological scaffolds—organized structure, not random noise.
- "Rich club" organization changes but networks remain structured.

**P6 support**: **P6-F2 contradicted** — integration/connectivity increases, not decreases.

---

### Barrett et al. (2020). *NeuroImage*, 209, 116539.

**Design**: Psilocybin and connectivity in default mode and salience networks.

**Findings**:
- Acute connectivity alterations; increased cross-network coupling in some measures.
- Subjective intensity correlates with network changes.

**P6 support**: Structured change correlates with phenomenal report—P6-F3 partial support.

---

### Tagliazucchi et al. (2014). *Human Brain Mapping*, 35(11), 5440–5458.

**Design**: BOLD signal diversity and entropy under LSD.

**Findings**:
- Increased signal diversity (entropy-related measures).
- Simultaneous increase in **functional connectivity** in some bands.

**Reconciliation**: Entropy and connectivity can co-increase—richness may be **reorganized integration**, not pure disorder.

---

## 3. Panpsychist Interpretation

### Integration Expansion Model (P6)

1. Proto-experiential properties are ubiquitous (A3).
2. Normal waking consciousness involves **high integration** with **exclusion** of most micro-structure (IIT exclusion postulate analog).
3. Psychedelics **relax exclusion** or **expand integration bandwidth**, allowing access to finer-grained experiential structure.
4. Subjective richness = broader integration, not random noise.

### Predictions

- Phenomenal richness should correlate with **both** increased connectivity AND specific integration metrics—not entropy alone.
- Micro-phenomenological reports (Petitmengin) should map to integration states.

### Falsifiers

- P6-F1: Only entropy increases, no connectivity/integration structure → *Partially contradicted* by Petri, Barrett.
- P6-F3: Richness uncorrelated with integration → *Insufficient data* for formal Φ during peak experience.

## 5. Research Gaps (Tier 3)

1. Simultaneous **formal Φ or PCI** + subjective micro-phenomenology during psychedelic peak.
2. Distinguish **entropy increase** from **integration reorganization** in same subjects.
3. Test whether richness reports track connectivity changes more than entropy changes.

---

## 6. Conclusion for Evidence Ledger

**P6 confidence**: *Moderate* — connectivity findings support integration expansion; entropic brain requires reconciliation, not rejection of P6.

**Recommended program stance**: Psychedelic states are **not** pure noise (P6-F1 weakened); panpsychist integration-expansion model remains viable pending direct Φ measurement.
