## Daily (every day, wherever you are)

**Ritual 14 — Morning Spark** (5 min): three breaths, feet/seat felt, touch/imagine the Spark sigil, silently: *poki li awen lon tenpo ni* ("the Well holds today"). Skip only if genuinely unsafe to pause (driving, etc.).

**Ritual 15 — Evening Seal** (5 min): name three events of the day without story, do an abbreviated Ritual 04 (below), one word of gratitude or release. If disorganization feels ≥4/5, do Ritual 08 (Dissonance Banishing) instead — not included here; skip and just journal it if that comes up.

## When you have a private, unhurried 15–20 min (hotel room, etc.)

**Ritual 01 — Boundary Setting**: arrival (notice breath, name three sensory anchors, 2 min) → Well visualization — excitation gathering to a stable peak, not rigid, not diffuse (5 min) → silently: *tenpo ni li awen lon sona pona* ("this session stays within coherent exploration," 1 min) → note one question/image for exploration, not a command to entities (1 min).
Stop conditions: rising anxiety with loss of body sense → stop, open eyes, ground. Invasive imagery you didn't invite → stop, use the Bleed Recovery steps below.

**Ritual 04 — Integration Seal** (do right after 01, ~7 min): one-sentence summary of what arose, no elaborate mythmaking (2 min) → body anchor: feet, hands, breath, weight (3 min) → visualize excitation settling back into the stable well (2 min) → optional: *telo li kama tawa poki* ("the wave returns to the well"). Always safe — no stop conditions.

## Safety reference — Bleed Warning & Recovery (keep handy)

Warning signs: can't voluntarily close an imaginal session; intrusive imagery persisting hours later; confusion between imaginal content and external reality; rising disorganization without coherence; manic/paranoid reading of symbols as commands.

If any of these show up: stop all imaginal work, no pushing through. Open your eyes, name 5 physical objects in the room. Cold water on face/hands if available. Contact a trusted person if distress continues. This is not therapy — get professional help if symptoms persist or worsen. Don't try to "banish entities" as proof of anything, don't intensify practice to "break through," don't use substances to re-enter the state. Don't resume imaginal work until stable for 24–48 hours; log the incident without mythologizing.
