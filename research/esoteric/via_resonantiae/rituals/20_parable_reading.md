# Ritual 20: Parable Reading

> **Epistemic Status:** Any grade. Pistis.

**Epistemic mode:** Pistis | **Duration:** 15 min

---

## Purpose

Recitation of myth and scripture for orientation—not doctrine exam.

## Steps

1. Select passage: Liber I parable, Liber II veil, or [`../cosmology/THE_MYTH.md`](../cosmology/THE_MYTH.md) excerpt
2. Read aloud slowly
3. One minute silence
4. Optional: one sentence personal resonance (not debate)

## Rotation

Weekly through five libers over five weeks.

Reading does not update evidence_ledger.
