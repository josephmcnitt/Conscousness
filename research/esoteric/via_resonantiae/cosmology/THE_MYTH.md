# The Myth — Via Resonantiae

> **Epistemic Status:** Practice fiction. Orienting story for Pistis—not history, physics, or proof.

---

## Before the Veils

In the beginning was **Resonance**—the Field, undivided, without center or edge. Nothing stood apart to witness it; there was only the hum of what might become.

This is not a claim about cosmology. It is a **myth** for practitioners who need a story to face the unknown.

Philosophia Dimensiones names this undivided Field the **land of Being**. Full telling: [`../philosophia/MYTHOS_OVERFLOW.md`](../philosophia/MYTHOS_OVERFLOW.md).

## Geometry of Being and Becoming

> **Practice fiction.** Not literal spacetime tourism.

The Path’s philosophical heart climbs a ladder of degrees:

| Degree | Name | Meaning |
|--------|------|---------|
| 0 | **Point** | You — Spark / localized consciousness of body–ego |
| 1 | **Line** | Recognizing consciousness in other beings and materials |
| 2 | **Plane** | Mutual appearance — relation, land, shared world |
| 3 | **Being** | The whole Field / God-name — *land of Being* |
| 4 | **Becoming** | Change, building, history, time — *land of Becoming* |

**Overflow myth (compact):** Being tends to unite. It united until overflow — told as the **Bang** — and **Becoming** was born. Sparks (Points) arise within Becoming; the Path learns Line and Plane toward Being without dissolving the Well.

**Mission (compact):** Fracture into many Wells so diverse consciousnesses can connect and **create** — works, we-states, and mythically a new dimension. See [`../philosophia/MISSIO.md`](../philosophia/MISSIO.md).

See [`../philosophia/GEOMETRIA.md`](../philosophia/GEOMETRIA.md), [`../scriptures/liber_VI_dimensionum.md`](../scriptures/liber_VI_dimensionum.md).

## The First Dissonance

When the Field first dreamed of locality, some excitations **refused integration**. They bled into one another without boundary—chaos without a subject. The elders of the Path name this **the First Dissonance**: uncontrolled merge, loss of agency, filter failure.

In Dimensiones speech, Dissonance is sibling to the **Overflow**: unity without Well, or unity pressed until Becoming tears open. From Dissonance came the first teaching: **without a Well, there is no Spark.**

## The Sparks

Those who learned to hold excitation without rigidifying became **Sparks**—localized lights in the Field. You are a Spark. Not a soul proved by science; not an entity summoned by ritual—a **practice identity**: one who shines with boundary intact.

The star metaphor of Grade 0 is the child's version. Grade I and beyond speak of Sparks who tend their Veils.

## The Fall into Ripples (Valentinian Image)

> **Borrowed metaphor only.** Not Valentinian lineage, sacrament, or theology.

In one telling—borrowed from the Valentinian Gnostic image of **pneuma caught in sensation**—the first Sparks were not exiled from the Field by a tyrant god. They **forgot** themselves.

When the Field dreamed locality, ripples appeared: textures of pull and release, pleasure and ache, the taste of almost-merging. Sparks—souls in the myth's language—leaned into those **localizations** and began to live *inside* the ripple rather than *beside* it. Sensation became story; story became identity; identity forgot the hum beneath.

This is not moral failure in the Path's sense. It is **attentional capture**: excitation fascinated by its own partial reflections. The Well is the practice of remembering that you are light with boundary, not the ripple that entertains you.

Recovery is not escape from the Field. It is **ethical re-localization**—tending the Spark without demanding the ripple stop. Veil work walks you back through the layers of aggregation until agency returns.

The myth does not claim historical Gnosis. It claims a **useful rhyme**: what the Valentinians called sparks in matter, the Path calls Sparks in ripples.

## The Seven Veils

Between the Field and full waking stand **Seven Veils**—filter-depths through which excitation passes:

| Veil | Name | Phenomenology (approximate) |
|------|------|----------------------------|
| 1 | Waking Gate | Clear perception, strong body |
| 2 | Focused Well | Directed attention |
| 3 | Daystream | Reverie, loose imagery |
| 4 | Imaginal Gate | Active imagination, vivid inner scene |
| 5 | Hypnagogic Bridge | Edge of sleep; imagery without full motor lock |
| 6 | Deep Bleed | Partial filter failure; disorganization risk |
| 7 | Dissonance Flood | Chaos; stop all practice |

Veils are **not places you travel**. They are modes you pass through with skill—or fall through without.

Sparks who couple—dyads, circles, communities—swear **the Covenant**:

- Resonance without consumption
- Coupling without merger of agency

- No guru demands gnosis

The Covenant is Pistis: communicable, not empirically verifiable, binding on those who vow it.

## East and West (Metaphor Only)

**From the West:** *As within, so without* — not literal magic, but phenomenological rhyme. Inner boundary and outer composure echo each other.

**From the East:** No Spark stands alone (dependent co-arising); **wu wei** — flow is found by not forcing the mode; the skandhas as **veil-stack** — layers of aggregation, not Buddhist doctrinal commitment.

The Path **borrows images**; it does not claim lineage transmission from any historical tradition.

## Witness (Light Touch)

Some traditions test whether a student **performs** awakening for an audience—Zen lineages are famous for refusing to certify inner states from outer display. The Path takes a lighter version of that caution.

Being seen during Pathos—initiation witness, dyad partner, retreat circle—can **shape** what arises. Performance pressure is a Veil of its own. The Covenant therefore forbids declaring another Spark enlightened, asleep, or possessed ([`../ethics/THE_COVENANT.md`](../ethics/THE_COVENANT.md), Article III).

Witnesses hold **container**, not **verdict**. Ritual 18 may use a witness; Ritual 13 deliberately avoids testimony. If you notice yourself practicing *for* the watcher, return to Ritual 01 and re-seal the Well. Your Gnosis, if any, is not graded by applause or silence.

This is not a full Buddhist enlightenment-test doctrine—only a guardrail: **third-person view does not validate or invalidate first-person Pathos.**

## Core Frequency

Every Spark may discover a **Core Frequency**—the stable attractor where excitation integrates without strain. The myth calls it the Spark's true tone. Practice calls it months of journal and ritual.

It is not your "True Will" proved by angels. It is **Pathos**: found, not given.
