# Ritual 05: Bleed Warning & Recovery

> **Epistemic Status:** Constructed safety protocol. Not evidence. When filter failure signs appear (P8).

---

## Purpose

Recognize and interrupt disorganized bleed. **Safety over exploration.**

---

## Warning Signs (Chaos Flood)

- Inability to close imaginal session voluntarily
- Persistent intrusive imagery hours after practice
- Confusion between imaginal content and external reality
- Rising disorganization without coherence
- Manic or paranoid interpretation of symbols as commands

---

## Immediate Recovery

1. **Stop** all imaginal work. No "pushing through."
2. **Open eyes.** Name 5 physical objects in the room.
3. **Cold water** on face or hands if available.
4. **Contact** trusted person if distress continues.
5. **Professional help** if symptoms persist or worsen—this protocol is not therapy.

---

## Do Not

- Attempt to "banish entities" as proof of occult cosmology
- Increase practice intensity to "break through"
- Use substances to re-enter state

---

## Research Note

Filter failure (P8) is high bleed + high disorganization + low organized phi. FEP treats this as **stop**, not initiation.

See [`../IMAGINATION_AND_THE_ASTRAL.md`](../IMAGINATION_AND_THE_ASTRAL.md) P8 boundary.

---

## After Recovery

Do not resume imaginal work until stable 24–48 hours. Log incident without mythologizing.
