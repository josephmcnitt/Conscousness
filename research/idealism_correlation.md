# Idealism Correlation: Analytic Idealism and BFC+FEO

Part IV document. **Correlation only**—the program retains A1 (physical structure is real).

---

## Thesis

Analytic idealism (Kastrup, 2019+) and the BFC+FEO stack describe **the same ontological picture in different vocabulary**, differing mainly on the status of physical structure.

## Where the Program Diverges (A1)

**Russellian panprotopsychism retains physical structure as real:**

- Physics describes genuine relational structure
- Intrinsic nature is experiential (A2)
- Matter is not *only* appearance from inside a dissociated alter

**I11 status:** Translation-equivalent for **subject formation**; not equivalent for **metaphysics of matter**.

---

## Shared Falsifiers

Both idealism and this program fail if:

| ID | Condition |
|----|-----------|
| G1 | Illusionism succeeds—no real qualia to localize or dissociate |
| G3 | Intrinsic nature definitively non-experiential |
| P9-F3 | Filter model redundant with IIT-only (no dissociation story needed) |

Idealism-specific: if physical structure is **empirically indispensable** in a way that cannot be treated as appearance without loss of explanatory power, pure analytic idealism weakens while Russellian monism may remain.
