# Ritual 03: Dyad Coupling

> **Epistemic Status:** Constructed practice. Not evidence. Not telepathy proof.

**Research analog:** P7 / P12 cross-coupling; coupled excitations.

---

## Purpose

Practice ethical synchrony with one partner—breath, attention, or creative improv.

---

## Prerequisites

Mutual consent, shared stop signal, Ritual 01 individually first.

---

## Steps

1. **Parallel boundary (3 min):** Each sets Well silently; face each other or share space.
2. **Breath entrainment (5–10 min):** Match inhale/exhale without forcing; allow drift and re-sync.
3. **Shared focus (5–15 min):** One word, tone, or improvised movement. No script required.
4. **Decouple (2 min):** Break synchrony deliberately; return to individual breath.

---

## Stop Conditions

- Either partner invokes stop signal
- Sense of merged identity without recovery → stop, separate, ground

---

## Expected Phenomenology

Increased we-feeling, timing alignment. **Not** proof of collective consciousness.

---

## After

Each logs: coupling sense (1–10), disorganization (1–10).
