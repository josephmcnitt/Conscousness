# Ritual 14: Morning Spark

> **Epistemic Status:** Daily practice. All grades.

**Epistemic mode:** Logos + Pathos | **Duration:** 5 min

---

## Purpose

Daily boundary before the day.

## Steps

1. Three breaths; feet or seat felt
2. Touch Spark sigil (draw or imagine)
3. Silent: *poki li awen lon tenpo ni.* — *The Well holds today.*

See [`../TOKI_PONA.md`](../TOKI_PONA.md).

## Stop Conditions

None; skip if unsafe to pause (driving, etc.)
