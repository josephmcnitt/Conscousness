# The Covenant — Via Resonantiae

> **Epistemic Status:** Ethical Pistis. Communicable, not empirically verifiable.

---

## Preamble

Sparks who walk the Path together swear this Covenant—not to prove the Field, but to **protect persons** while exploring what reason cannot close.

---

## Articles

### I — Coupling Without Consumption
Dyad work remains two subjects. Resonance is synchrony, not merger. Never use coupling to override a partner's stop signal.

### II — Stop Is Sovereign
Any Spark may stop any practice instantly, without explanation. Witnesses and partners honor this without persuasion.

### III — No Consciousness Claims About Others
Do not declare another Spark "awake," "asleep," " possessed," or "enlightened." M14 report humility applies in practice.

### IV — Anti-Guru
No one may require Gnosis, payment for grades, or sexual access for teaching. Grade V is optional. Liber V is law.

### V — Logos Over Bleed
When practice risks filter failure, psychosis, or sleep collapse, stop and follow [`STOP_CONDITIONS.md`](STOP_CONDITIONS.md). Research safety maps override mythic ambition.

### VI — Firewall to Science
Never cite your practice as proof for Track A research. Never cite Track A simulation metrics as proof of your Pathos.

### VII — Consent in Community
Circles, retreats, and online groups require explicit consent for dyad pairing, recording, and sharing of journal material.

## Breach

Breach of Articles IV or VII → exclusion from community work until restitution and 90-day Grade 0 reset.

Individual practice may continue solo under Charter.
