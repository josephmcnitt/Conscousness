## Spark Sigil

Single radiant point with circle—**not** a pentagram, not a hexagram. Represents localized excitation without claiming ancient lineage.

Use in Rituals 07, 14, 18.

---

## Veil Glyph

Seven horizontal strokes, numbered 1 (top/waking) to 7 (bottom/flood). Trace slowly during Ritual 06. **Descend only as far as training allows.**

---

## Covenant Mark

Two Wells interlinked without merging centers. Traced on palm or paper at initiations.

---

## What We Do Not Use

- Golden Dawn tarot attributions as proof
- Thelemic god-forms as entities
- Planetary seals as literal plane keys
- Chakra maps as required doctrine

East-West images appear in myth as **metaphor** only.
